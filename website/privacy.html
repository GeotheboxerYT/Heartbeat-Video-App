Privacy

Your training data should stay understandable.

This page explains the basic privacy approach for Ticker Flip during beta development. Last updated July 20, 2026.

Data the app may collect

Ticker Flip may store account details, profile details, workout videos, heart-rate samples, PVT results, sleep metrics, journal entries, and technical sync information.

How it is used

The data is used to show your workout review, heart-rate charts, session summaries, PVT comparisons, sleep readiness estimates, and journal history.

Local storage

Videos and session data may be stored on your device so you can review them later. Deleting the app may delete app-local data from the device.

Cloud sync

When sync is enabled, session data may be sent to the Ticker Flip API at api.tickerflip.com. Video sync may be limited or disabled while the product is being tested.

Sharing

Ticker Flip does not need to sell personal training data to work. If third-party services are used for hosting, authentication, analytics, or storage, they are used to operate the app.

Health disclaimer

Heart-rate, sleep, and readiness information can be useful for training review, but it is not medical advice. Talk to a medical professional for health concerns.

Contact

For privacy questions or deletion requests, contact support@tickerflip.com.
