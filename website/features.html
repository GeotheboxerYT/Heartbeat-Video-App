Feature map

Every rep gets a timestamp.

Ticker Flip is built around one simple idea: if effort matters, you should be able to look back and see when it happened.

Video

Workout video + BPM overlay

Record with the iPhone camera and keep the heart-rate counter tied to the same timeline.

HR

Heart-rate only mode

Skip video when you only want the chart, BPM history, and session summary.

BLE

Heart-rate strap pairing

Scan for devices that advertise the standard heart-rate service so random Bluetooth devices stay out of the way.

PvP

Participant profiles

Assign people to straps, label their lines, and compare effort without confusing device names.

PVT

Before and after PVT

Compare reaction speed, misses, false starts, and lapses before and after hard training.

Sleep

Sleep and readiness

Review sleep length, efficiency, wake time, resting heart rate, and recovery score together.

Bell

Interval alerts

Optional round timer with loud bell sounds for work and rest blocks.

Log

Training journal

Auto-fill what the app already knows, then add food, sleep, training notes, and context.

Sync

Cloud-ready API

Sessions can sync to the live Ticker Flip backend when internet access is available.

Intensity zones

Color-coded effort.

The app uses clear BPM ranges to make intensity easier to read during recording and review.

121-141Light building work
141-162Strong aerobic push
