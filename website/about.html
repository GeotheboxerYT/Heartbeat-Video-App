About

Built for the part of training you replay.

Ticker Flip is for athletes, coaches, and people who want more than a final average BPM number. It is for checking the exact moments where form, fatigue, and effort collide.

Why it exists

Most workout apps tell you what happened after the fact. Ticker Flip is being built to show where it happened. The goal is simple: pair your video, heart rate, PVT readiness, sleep, and journal notes so training review feels concrete.

What it is not

Ticker Flip is not a medical device, not a diagnosis tool, and not a replacement for a doctor, coach, or emergency service. It is a training review and performance-awareness app.

Where it is now

The iPhone app is in active testing. Public download links are not available yet, but the backend and website are being prepared for outside testers.
